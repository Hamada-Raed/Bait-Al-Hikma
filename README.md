### What We Offer

Bait Al-Hikma serves as a bridge between learners and educators, providing a complete ecosystem for online education. Whether you're a school student seeking help with your curriculum, a university student looking to supplement your studies, or an educator wanting to share your expertise, our platform offers the tools and resources you need to excel.

#### For Students

- **Personalized Learning Paths**: Courses tailored to your grade level, country, track, and major ensure that you're learning content that's relevant to your educational journey
- **Flexible Scheduling**: Learn at your own pace with courses designed to fit your schedule - no more rushing to keep up or waiting for others
- **Interactive Content**: Engage with multimedia courses, video lessons, and interactive quizzes that make learning enjoyable and effective
- **Private Tutoring**: Book one-on-one sessions with qualified teachers based on your availability. Get personalized attention when you need it most
- **Progress Tracking**: Monitor your learning journey and achievements as you complete courses. See how far you've come and what's next
- **Diverse Subjects**: Access courses covering everything from school curriculum to university-level subjects across various majors and tracks

#### For Teachers

- **Course Creation Tools**: Build comprehensive courses with chapters, videos, and quizzes. Share your knowledge with students around the world
- **Availability Management**: Set your schedule and let students book private lessons at times that work for you
- **Student Engagement**: Track student progress and provide personalized feedback to help them succeed
- **Flexible Pricing**: Set your own rates for private lessons based on your expertise and experience
- **Quality Assurance**: All courses go through an approval process, ensuring that your content meets high educational standards
- **Global Reach**: Teach students from different countries and educational systems, expanding your impact

#### For Administrators

- **Platform Management**: Oversee all courses, users, and content to ensure a smooth learning experience for everyone
- **Quality Control**: Review and approve courses before they go live, maintaining the platform's educational excellence
- **User Management**: Manage student and teacher accounts, ensuring a safe and productive learning environment
- **Analytics**: Monitor platform usage and educational outcomes to continuously improve the learning experience
